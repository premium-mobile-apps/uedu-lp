About the UEDU Platform

At UEDU, we understand the challenges that come with self-preparation or preparing your child for their academic future. That’s why our cohort-based learning platform focuses on building a bridge for communication between parents, schools and of course students. With the UEDU platform, everyone knows exactly what to do.


UEDU believes learning online shouldn’t compromise retention of skills, it should enhance them. We break traditional learning barriers with virtual reality Campus Tours, augmented reality Educator Holograms and other immsersive mixed reality classroom experiences that help keep students engaged. Our platform provides the tools for real world experiences so that students can focus on learning.
